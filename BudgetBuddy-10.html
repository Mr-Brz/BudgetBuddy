Welcome to BudgetBuddy!
Your personal finance companion. Let's take a quick tour so you know exactly how to use it. It only takes a minute!
🏠
Dashboard
Your home screen. See your monthly income, expenses and net balance at a glance. Charts show your spending over time and by category. The simulator lets you see how much you'd save by cutting a category.
💸
Transactions
Add your income and expenses here. Give each one a label, amount, category and date. Toggle "Recurring monthly" for bills that repeat every month — BudgetBuddy will remind you when they're due.
👛
Budget Envelopes
Set a monthly spending limit per category. A progress bar fills as you spend — it turns red if you go over. This is the best way to stop overspending in any area.
🎯
Savings Goals
Set a savings goal with a target amount and date. Add funds to it as you save. The progress bar fills up as you get closer — satisfying to watch grow!
⚙️
Settings
Set your name for a personalised greeting, switch between light and dark mode, choose your currency, manage categories, and export your data as CSV anytime.
🚀
You're all set!
Start by adding your first transaction. If you ever need this tutorial again, you can find it in Settings. Good luck with your finances!
